# Seeger Framework

Version: v0.1-draft
Status: Working Draft

## Purpose

This document defines the legal-positioning layer used to explain sincerely held belief, identity recognition, and institutional classification within OneGodian Protocol™ documentation.

## Core Standard

The Seeger framework asks whether a belief occupies a place in the life of the possessor parallel to that filled by God in traditionally religious persons.

## OneGodian Application

The OneGodian framework should be documented through:

- sincerity of belief
- consistency of expression
- written declarations
- historical authorship record
- institutional documentation
- voluntary participation
- clear separation between identity, governance, and commercial operations

## Required Elements for Documentation

1. Statement of sincere belief
2. Personal meaning and role in life
3. Evidence of consistency over time
4. Supporting records or publications
5. Institutional or community context, if applicable
6. Clear statement that Gregorian/civil law remains controlling for legal matters

## Risk Controls

Avoid overstating legal conclusions. This document is a framework for documentation and explanation, not a substitute for legal advice or court determination.

## Repository Role

This file supports legal clarity, belief-mapping documentation, declaration tools, and public-facing institutional explanation.
